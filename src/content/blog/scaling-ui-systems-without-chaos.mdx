Most UI systems fail when they optimize for consistency and forget adaptability.

I prefer a composable approach:

## Clear primitives

Buttons, cards, and badges should be intentionally small, style-safe primitives.

## Smart composition

Feature components should compose primitives with local context, rather than adding one-off variants globally.

## Tokenized customization

Color, spacing, and elevation should be controlled by design tokens. This keeps rebrands and theme work fast.

When teams follow this model, product velocity stays high and visual quality remains coherent.
